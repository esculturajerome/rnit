On January 7, 2025, the Barangay Officials of Poblacion, Alcantara, Romblon, conducted a clean-up drive targeting the facade and entrance of the Romblon National Institute of Technology (RNIT). The activity focused on clearing the roadside and areas surrounding the main gate to ensure a tidy environment for students, employees, and passersby.

During the drive, officials concentrated on trimming overgrown grass and weeds along the perimeter fence. Using grass cutters and manual tools, they cleared vegetation and gathered debris into sacks for disposal.
