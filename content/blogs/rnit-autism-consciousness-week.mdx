The Romblon National Institute of Technology (RNIT), in partnership with Tugdan Elementary School and the Rural Health Unit (RHU) of Alcantara, successfully conducted Autism Consciousness Week from January 30 to February 3, 2026. The program promoted awareness, understanding, and inclusive support for children on the autism spectrum and those with special needs.
